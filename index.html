Get Ready to TangleDown

tangledown combines the simplicity of Markdown syntax with the sexiness of Tangle.js reactive documents and the power of SymPy symbolic solvers.

You type

If you eat [tk:TKAdjustableNumber cookies][#cookies], you consume [tk:TKAdjustableNumber calories][#calories]. This constitutes [tk:TKAdjustableNumber %][#dailypercent] of a daily intake of [tk:TKAdjustableNumber calories][#dailycalories].

[#dailycalories]: #dailycalories:init=2100
[#dailypercent]: #dailypercent:init=100:constraint=Math.floor(100*#calories/#dailycalories)
[#cookies]: #cookies:init=3:constraint=#calories/50
[#calories]: #calories:init=150:constraint=#cookies*50

You get

If you eat tk:TKAdjustableNumber cookies, you consume tk:TKAdjustableNumber calories. This constitutes tk:TKAdjustableNumber % of a daily intake of tk:TKAdjustableNumber calories.
